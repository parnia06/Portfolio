how to cook?

Combine orange juice, lemon juice, and lime juice for marinade in a large glass or ceramic bowl.
Add cilantro, soy sauce, garlic, chili powder, cumin, paprika, black pepper, chipotle pepper,
and oregano; stir to combine.Slowly whisk in olive oil until well combined. Remove 1 cup of the
marinade and place in a small bowl; cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for use after the steak
is cooked.Place steak between two sheets of heavy plastic (resealable freezer bags work well) on a solid,
level surface. Firmly pound steak with the smooth side of a meat mallet to a thickness of 1/4 inch.After
pounding, poke steak all over with a fork. Place steak in the marinade in the large bowl, cover, and marinate
in the refrigerator for 24 hours.When ready to cook, preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat, and lightly
oil the grate.Remove steak from the marinade and shake off excess. Discard the remaining marinade.Cook steak
on the preheated grill to desired doneness, about 5 minutes per side for medium-rare.Place on a serving platter
and pour the reserved, unused marinade over top. Serve immediately.
